Android Development

 

Web Spidola has been developed in 2016. It is a web radio tuner, recorder and player
It is no longer available for download via the Google Play Store.

Reason for Discontinuation:

During a routine policy review, Google requested that the application provide individual licensing documentation for every podcast RSS feed indexed by the app’s scanner. As a standard podcast aggregator, the app utilizes publicly available RSS feeds—the industry standard for open distribution.
Due to the impractical nature of obtaining thousands of individual licenses for public content that is intended for open syndication, I have chosen to discontinue official distribution on the Play Store.
